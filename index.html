Consequences of Late Payment of Fees:

If fees are not paid by the specified deadline, the following consequences may occur:

  • Penalties or late fees may be imposed.
  • Access to certain campus facilities or services may be restricted.
  • Registration for future classes or academic activities may be blocked.
  • Transcripts or academic records may be withheld.

It is important to ensure timely payment of fees to avoid these consequences.
